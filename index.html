About Me

My name is Andrew Harringa, and am currently studying at the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp to become a full stack web developer. I am a graduate from the University of Iowa, with a Bachelor of Arts in Art with a focus in Graphic Design. With many hours logged on the computer for design projects, I feel I have a great grasp on a great user environment, as well as an aesthetically pleasing experience. With this specialty, I plan to breathe life into my current degree and expand further into the capabilities of technology as we see it today. I am very excited to see what the future holds, also who and what I'll be working with in the future. I hope you enjoy my site!
